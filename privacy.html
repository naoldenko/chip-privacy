1. Introduction

Welcome to chip ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Our Commitment: Your privacy is fundamental to our mission of providing a safe, secure, founder-focused companion for entrepreneurs to use. We believe in transparency and giving you control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Account Data: Account creation date and last update timestamps

Health and Wellness Data

  • Mood Check-ins: Daily mood ratings (1-5 scale) and associated notes
  • Community Posts: Posts you share in community categories (gratitude, reflection, growth)
  • Community Interactions: Replies and responses to community posts

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics
  • Session Data: Login/logout times and session duration
  • Error Logs: Technical errors and app performance issues

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Services: Authentication, database storage, analytics, and file storage
  • OpenAI API: AI-powered mood forecasting and content moderation
  • Perplexity API: AI-powered search
  • Google Fonts: Typography services

3. How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your user account
  • Mood Tracking: Storing and analyzing your daily mood check-ins
  • Community Features: Enabling social interactions and content sharing
  • AI Advisor: Providing personalized entrepreneur-focused advice

Service Improvement

  • Analytics: Understanding app usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Performance Monitoring: Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Feature Development: Developing new features based on user behavior

Content Moderation

  • Community Safety: Using AI-powered moderation to ensure community guidelines compliance
  • Inappropriate Content Detection: Filtering content that violates our community standards

Communication

  • Account Notifications: Important account-related communications
  • Service Updates: Information about app updates and new features

4. Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Encrypted database storage
  • Firebase Storage: Secure file storage for profile images
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure login using Firebase Authentication
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Firebase, OpenAI, Perplexity) that help us operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

Community Content

Posts and replies you share in the community are visible to other users, but your personal information (email, profile details) remains private.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: You may access your mood check-ins, community posts, and profile information through the app.
  • Update Information: You can modify your profile and account settings at any time.
  • Delete Account: You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us or using in-app features (if available).
  • Export Data: You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after extended periods of inactivity.

International User Rights

  • GDPR (EU): If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your data, request data portability, and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
  • CCPA (California): California residents have the right to know, access, delete, and opt out of the sale of their personal information.

Breach Notification

If we become aware of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:

  • Consent: For optional features and analytics
  • Contract Performance: To provide the core app functionality
  • Legitimate Interest: For service improvement and security
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

You have the right to withdraw consent, object to processing, and request data portability or erasure under applicable data protection laws.
